## Problem

Raw sensor exports were difficult for non-specialists to interpret, especially when teams needed to explain findings to community partners in a short presentation window.

## Solution

RiverWatch turns time-series data into a focused narrative: current status, recent anomalies, and site-to-site comparison. The interface centers on whether the stream conditions are stable, improving, or worth investigating further.

## Notable features

- Multi-site charts with clear comparison states.
- Outlier callouts that support presentation storytelling.
- Export-friendly screenshots for poster sessions and reports.
