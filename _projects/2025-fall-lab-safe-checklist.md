## Problem

Lab safety checklists were being completed inconsistently because the paper forms were easy to ignore, difficult to archive, and disconnected from room-specific instructions.

## Solution

The team built a responsive checklist flow that adapts by room type, highlights unresolved hazards, and generates a simple completion log. The design emphasizes speed at the bench instead of a dense form experience.

## Outcomes

- Faster task completion during pilot tests.
- Better visibility into incomplete steps.
- Cleaner handoff between student workers and supervising staff.
