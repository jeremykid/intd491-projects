## Problem

Students reported that existing booking tools showed room availability, but not the context needed to decide quickly which space actually fit their group. The team focused on shortening that decision loop.

## Solution

The prototype pairs a searchable campus map with filters for elevator access, screen-sharing equipment, room size, and quiet-zone status. Each pod card surfaces the information most students said they wanted in the first ten seconds of a search.

## What to look at

- The booking-ready pod cards that combine logistics, location, and accessibility details.
- The simplified filter set designed around common student questions instead of administrative categories.
- The poster for the research framing, interview summary, and usability test takeaways.
